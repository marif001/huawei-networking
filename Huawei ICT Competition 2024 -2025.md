# Details

**Higher Education Commission (HEC) | HUAWEI**

Huawei ICT Competition 2024-25 Middle East & Central Asia

The Huawei ICT Competition for the Middle East and Central Asia, presented in partnership with UNESCO IITE (Institute for Information Technologies in Education) is dedicated to advancing digital learning and fostering collaboration between industry and education. This annual ICT talent exchange event is specifically designed for students, offering a prestigious platform to showcase their talents and gain worldwide recognition for their achievements.
Last Date to Apply: 18 October, 2024 

**Details:** https://www.hec.gov.pk/english/HECAnnouncements/Pages/Huawei-ICT-Competition.aspx
